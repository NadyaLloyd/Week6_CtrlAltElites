ToDo Zoom University

Why do we need this application?

With virtual learning comes inevitable procrastination. BAD! Having a checklist corresponding to your assigned Loyola courses will benefit your learning experience and aid you in your future endevours.

What can you, as the user, modify?

  1. Course Specifications
  2. Semester Overview
  3. Assignments
  4. Project
  5. Due Dates
  6. Comments
  7. Ranking System

KEEP TRACK

With Virtual Learning it can be difficult to keep track of all the little assignments, and although there are many task trackers available on your laptop/appstore, we will find a way to make our application unique to Loyola. The user will first list out their courses, then week by week will continuously update their assignments in the form of checklists. We will grant user satisfaction each time a task is completed (still figuring out what we want to do for that). The application will provide the user a simple, yet innovative platform to manage their workload.

FOCUS

Make a priority list for yourself:

  • What assignments are most important short term/long term?
  • What projects are most important short term/long term?
  • Are your priorities alligned with the assigned due dates?

Why Loyola?

Loyola University provides a foundation for the future success of its students. With the recent transition to online due to COVID-19, both professors and students have been impacted. Our focus will rely on the impact students face with the lack of on campus resources and time management consequences.
